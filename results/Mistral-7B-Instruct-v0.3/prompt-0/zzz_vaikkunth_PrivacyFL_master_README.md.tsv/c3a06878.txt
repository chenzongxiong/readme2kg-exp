`Personal computation time` indicates how long your training took for that iteration while `<SOFTWARE>Simulated time to receive federated weights</SOFTWARE>` takes into account user-defined communication latencies between the clients and the server, as well as how long it took the other clients to compute their weights and the server to average them.
